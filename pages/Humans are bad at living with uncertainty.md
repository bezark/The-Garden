This was something I really felt during [[COVID]]: when we all had to gauge whether or not we were exposed we all struggled. We found it very hard to both accept the possibility that we could be infected even though there wasn't any visible evidence. It was hard to live in a story of [[Quantum]] state where it was both true and not true that we were infected. 

Perhaps this is not true for ALL humans, that's a sweeping generalization that I don't get to make, but it certainly rang true for my and the people i observed in the place and time of the pandemic. 

For me it is also a source of deep and personal [[anxiety]]
